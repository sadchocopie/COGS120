FAQ

1. Where are the items on LineUp from?

We gathered items for comparisons from some of the most popular online shopping sites. Currently, we have Amazon, Walmart, Target, eBay, Macy's, and Best Buy.

2. Do I pay through LineUp for the items?

No, LineUp only provides recommendation of the best item to purchase based on our data analysis. All transactions will be done on Amazon, Walmart, Target, eBay, Macy's, or Best Buy.

3. What is the data analysis approach?

We use a unique approach that takes into account prices of items. In addition, reviews and comments will be utilized in the future versions.

4. How can I contact LineUp?

We have our contact information under our Contact Us page.
